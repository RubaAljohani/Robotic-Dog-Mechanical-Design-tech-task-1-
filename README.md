# Robotic-Dog-Mechanical-Design-tech-task-1-
Initial mechanical design of a simple robotic dog using Tinkercad
## Design Description

The robot has two body parts connected by a middle body joint.  

The robot has four legs. Each leg includes an thigh part, a leg part, and a knee joint.  
The legs are connected to the body using shoulder and hip joints.

## Main Components

- Front and rear body parts
- body middel joint
- Four legs
- Shoulder and hip joints
- Knee joints
- Wide feet for it to be stable
- Center of Gravity pointer


## Stability and Center of Gravity

The Center of Gravity (CG) is placed near the middle of the body between the four legs.  
This helps distribute the weight more evenly and reduces the chance of the robot falling while standing or walking.

The feet are wide in order to increase the contact area with the ground and make it stable.


## Tool Used

Tinkercad was used to create the 3D mechanical design.
